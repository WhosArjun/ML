Random Forest Classifcation : The order of features chosen might lead to huge difference in the outcome. You create mutliple descision trees and train all of them
onto the same data. In the end you get a new test example, and feed the test example into all of the descision trees.
The collective/majority of the votes wins and that has the most probability of being a yes or a no.
